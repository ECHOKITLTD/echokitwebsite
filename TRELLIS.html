Calm, Private Spaces for the Modern Office

Beyond spatial organisation, TRELLIS manages noise levels, creating a focused, calm ambiance.

Elevate your workspace with this sustainable choice, transforming any area into a haven of productivity and well-being.

HOOP

Hoop makes a bold statement through large circular perforations arranged in a confident grid.

The design feels open, graphic, and contemporary, offering a distinctive divider that balances strong visual identity with acoustic comfort.

DOT

Dot is the most minimal of the collection, using evenly spaced small perforations for a clean, timeless look.

Its restrained geometry suits interiors that need subtle acoustic performance without overpowering the overall scheme.

DIGITAL

Digital interprets the divider as a pixel-like field of small circular perforations, creating a modern tech-inspired surface.

It feels precise yet playful, ideal for collaborative environments, education spaces, and contemporary commercial interiors.

BALE 

Bale features clean vertical slots in a disciplined grid, giving it a calm architectural rhythm.

Its linear design feels modern and ordered, making it ideal for refined acoustic separation in offices and shared spaces.

BATCH

Batch offers a more playful linear composition, with staggered slot lengths that create movement across the panel.

It combines acoustic function with a subtle graphic character suited to creative, contemporary interiors.

KADO 

Kado brings a softer, more organic feel to acoustic zoning, with petal-like cut-outs that create lightness and pattern while helping manage reverberation in open interiors, studios, workspaces, and hospitality settings.

KIRIN

Kirin uses a denser floral-inspired pattern to bring decoration and softness into a room.

 The repeated organic cut-outs add visual texture while maintaining an elegant, welcoming feel for acoustic screening applications.
