Biophilic design, easy Install

Select from our range of seven distinctive designs, each inspired by nature, Viola, Foxtail, Sun Flower, Dandelion, Sweet Pea, Poppy and Orchid.

KANOPY is easy-to-install. The tenticular design and dimensions of KANOPY tiles enable an effortless install into 600mm a T-grid ceiling systems, with no additional accessories required.

Viola

Embodies the graceful elegance of Viola blooms, while delivering exceptional acoustic performance.

Foxtail 

Inspired by delicate foxtails, this design infuses a sense of whimsy while actively dampening sound

Sunflower

Create an elegant statement whilst benefiting from sound-absorption qualities.

Dandelion 

Has an airy aesthetic while ensuring optimal sound control.

Sweetpea

Bring the softness of Sweet Pea flowers into your space, creating an environment of beauty and acoustic tranquillity.

Poppy 

Taking the boldness of poppies these tiles make a statement, enhancing acoustics with grace

Orchid

Captures the timeless beauty and elegance of orchids, these tiles elevate your space with both aesthetics and acoustic performance
