BARK

Inspired by Nature. Grounded in Science.

BARK is a unique wall tile designed to enhance acoustic performance and create a textural backdrop.

Inspired by the organic contours of our native trees, BARK embodies a sense of tranquillity and well-being, echoing the calming essence of nature.

Committed to sustainability, BARK is made with 75% recycled material, aligning with our core values of eco-conscious design and responsible manufacturing practices.

Acoustic performance 

Made from sustainable PET Felt, the panel’s porous structure helps reduce echo and improve speech clarity. The angular profile diffuses soundwaves across multiple frequencies, breaking up reflections while adding a bold, architectural texture to interiors. Available in 11 PET Felt colours, it offers both acoustic comfort and design versatility
